About me

Hello! I'm Sonia Romero, a photographer and video producer based in Ottawa, ON Canada.

Ever since I was a child, I would grab my family's camcorder and create movies. Later I purchased my first DSLR camera and started taking photos and videos of everyone and everything.

My passion is capturing everyday moments and transforming them into an everlasting memory. I truly believe an image (or video) is worth more than a thousand words!

I was born in Mexico and I'm a mom of a toddler. Also, I'm currently studying at Algonquin College.
